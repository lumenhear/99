About Us

Clinic Image

At LUMEN Speech and Hearing Care, we are dedicated to providing exceptional, personalized care to individuals with communication challenges. Our team of licensed and certified speech-language pathologists and audiologists is committed to helping you achieve your communication goals.

We utilize the latest technology and evidence-based practices to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients. Our compassionate approach focuses on building trusting relationships and creating a supportive environment for growth and healing.

Whether you're seeking help for yourself or a loved one, we're here to guide you through every step of your journey to better communication.
